FAQ

Questions before you join?

How does it work?

Drop a tablet into your lunch box with a little water, close it, shake gently, and let the dissolving action help loosen odor and residue until a full wash is available.

Does it require a lot of water?

No. tiffytab is designed for a small amount of water so it can be used during the day without needing a full sink setup.

Is it easy to carry?

Yes. The tablets are compact and made for bags, desks, cars, lockers, travel kits, and everyday lunch routines.
